Parsee is a paid Mac App Store app. Pricing is shown in your local currency on the App Store listing.

Requires macOS 13 or later. Apple Silicon is recommended for the best performance.

Where can I send feedback?

You can reach the developer via the support link on the Mac App Store page or by email at support@parseejson.app.
